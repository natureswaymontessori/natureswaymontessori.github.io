Welcome To The Site!

At the AC, we have quite a different program compared to other schools, students operate a farm and coresponding farm stand, to make money for the school's microeconomy. The microeconomy is a middle school program that is controlled and managed by students, and includes arts and crafts, the farm, the farm stand, chickens, and business and marketing.
